EasyGrocery is a trusted online grocery platform established in 2015 with a mission to make everyday shopping simple, fast, and reliable for households across Kerala. What began as a small digital initiative has now grown into a complete statewide grocery network serving thousands of customers daily.

We specialize in delivering fresh groceries, household essentials, vegetables, fruits, packaged foods, and personal care products directly to customers’ doorsteps. Our strong logistics network spans across all districts in Kerala, ensuring accessibility and convenience for both urban and rural communities.

Our Reach & Delivery Promise

  • 1-Hour Express Delivery in major cities
  • 24-Hour Delivery anywhere in Kerala
  • Real-time order tracking
  • Reliable packaging and handling

We understand that groceries are an everyday necessity, so our goal is to deliver quickly without compromising freshness or quality.

Quality You Can Trust

Quality is the heart of EasyGrocery. Every product goes through a custom quality sorting process before dispatch. Our team carefully inspects fruits, vegetables, and essentials to ensure customers receive only the best.

  • Handpicked farm-fresh produce
  • Verified suppliers and brands
  • Hygienic packaging standards
  • Quality checks at multiple stages

Our Vision

To become Kerala’s most dependable online grocery partner by combining technology, logistics, and customer trust.

Our Mission

  • Make grocery shopping effortless and time-saving
  • Deliver fresh, high-quality products consistently
  • Build a strong statewide delivery ecosystem
  • Provide excellent customer service and support

Why Choose EasyGrocery?

  • Established and experienced since 2015
  • Complete Kerala delivery network
  • Fast city delivery within 1 hour
  • 24-hour service across the state
  • Carefully sorted, high-quality groceries
  • Affordable pricing and wide product range

At EasyGrocery, we don’t just deliver groceries — we deliver convenience, freshness, and reliability to every home in Kerala.
